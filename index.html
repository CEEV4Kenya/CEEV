Citizen E-Participation
in Electoral Verification

CEEV enables citizens to verify electoral results in real time — photograph your ballot, submit via WhatsApp, and watch a citizen-driven tally (DEW Tally) build alongside the official IEBC count.

Research Foundation

"Limited research addresses how citizen e-participation architectures can be designed to strengthen verification, transparency, and trust in fragile electoral processes." — CEEV Research Abstract, 2025

How It Works
01

Vote & Photograph

After voting, photograph your completed ballot showing all 6 positions clearly.

02

Send via WhatsApp

Send the photo to +254 114 748 864. Include your polling station name.

03

AI Extraction

Our AI reads your vote data, generates a unique CEV reference code, and sends you a receipt.

04

Verify & Confirm

Log in with your reference code to confirm your submission is correctly recorded.

05

Watch Dashboard

Compare the DEW citizen tally with IEBC official announcements live.

06

Flag Discrepancies

If tallies diverge beyond the margin, CEEV alerts observers to investigate.
